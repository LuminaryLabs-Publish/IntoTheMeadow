## Main finding

`resize()` samples live CSS size and clamps DPR to 1 through 2 inside every render call. It directly changes `canvas.width` and `canvas.height`, then derives the GL viewport and camera aspect from the requested values. The renderer does not query or expose actual drawing-buffer dimensions, WebGL limits, pixel budget, resize generation, fallback tier, surface revision or typed allocation result.

The editor independently reads viewport values and captures the canvas while attaching a renderer snapshot that contains no surface identity. A screenshot can therefore be reported without proof that its dimensions, projection and renderer state came from the same committed frame.
